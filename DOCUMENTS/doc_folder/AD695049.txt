I am not, nor have I ever pretended to be, an expert on microfiche.  Nevertheless, when I was invited to address the Third Annual Northeastern DDC/Industry Users Conference in Waltham, Massachusetts in April of 1968 I had the temerity to attempt to describe what I as a user would like to have in a fiche reader.  ("Towards a Uniform Federal Report Numbering System and a Cuddly Microfiche Reader--Two Modest Proposals." Revised September 1968.  AD-669204)
