A collection of 144 general reference works was analyzed and encoded according to 254 identifiable characteristics of services and contained data, comprising an approach language expressing search parametrs.. In response to a request submitted at on-line terminal, the REFSEARCH system retrieves the names of those works who profiles meet or exceed the specification.. The background and rationale of the system is discussed, its current use in the School of Librarianship, University of California, and its potential for direct service to library patrons..
